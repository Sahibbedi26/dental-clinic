Know Before You Book

Online dentist vs. in-chair visit

We're upfront about what a screen can and can't do. Here's the honest split.

Good fit for a virtual visit

  • Sudden tooth or gum pain — figuring out urgency
  • Second opinion on a treatment plan you were quoted
  • Follow-up after a filling, extraction, or surgery
  • Questions about whitening, sensitivity, or bad breath
  • Prescription for antibiotics ahead of an in-person fix
  • Guidance for a child's loose tooth or minor injury

Needs an in-person chair

  • Fillings, crowns, extractions, or root canals
  • Professional cleanings and deep scaling
  • X-rays and physical imaging
  • Braces, aligners, or any hands-on orthodontic work
  • Dental emergencies involving heavy bleeding or trauma
  • Anything requiring anesthesia

Think of Cusp as your first stop, not your only stop — we triage, advise, and refer you to trusted local clinics when hands need to be in your mouth.
